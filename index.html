Yae Miko's Specialty: Fukuuchi Udon

Ingredient Amount
Glutinous Rice Flour 50 grams
Water 350 mL
Red Food Coloring
Clear Food Wrap
Cooking Oil 1 tbsp
Tofu 2 packets
Black Sesame Paste
Barbecue Sauce
Udon Noodles 1 serving
Bonito Soup Stock 1 packet
Light Soy Sauce 15 mL
Mirin (Sweet Sake) 20 mL
Salt 1 pinch
Spring Onion
Sakura Cookie Cutter

Steps

  1. Mix 50g of glutinous rice flour with 50mL of water in a bowl. Knead until solid, then set half aside for later.
  2. Place a drop of red food coloring into the remaining half. Knead until the flour is pink.
  3. Use the food wrap to flatten a sheet of flour, then use the cookie cutter to form a pink sakura flower.
  4. Cut out the inside of the flower, then fill it in with the white flour from step 1.
  5. Use a small knife to cut the pink and white flour into sakura petal shapes.
  6. Simmer the sakura decorations on medium heat until solidified.
